## Deep Dive
Lightning uses off-chain channels and routed payments while relying on Bitcoin for final enforceability through timelocked settlement transactions.

**Real Data:** Show `on-chain fee recommendations` and `Lightning channel graph activity`. This helps learners see when L2 routing is economically attractive.

## Walkthrough
1. Open channel on-chain.
2. Route payments off-chain.
3. Rebalance or update channels as needed.
4. Close or settle on-chain when necessary.

## Common Misconceptions
- “Lightning replaces Bitcoin base layer.” It does not.
- “Lightning is unsafe for any real use.” Incorrect with proper operational controls.
- “All Lightning payments are always instant.” Routing and liquidity can fail.
